Pollution

Ocean Pollution For many years’ we as humans have been polluting our oceans in many ways from dumping toxic waste To dumping Plastics and other non-biodegradables waste into our oceans. Right now ocean trash poses one of the biggest dangers to both humans and ocean life, because plastics and other non-bio-degradable waste.
Plastics Plastics are major problem because they can take up to a 1000yrs for them to break down and dissolve https://www.weforum.org/agenda/2018/11/chart-of-the-day-this-is-how-long-everyday-plastic-items-last-in-the-ocean/ during that period fish can become tangled in them and become injured or die. Another problem is that plastic bags can be mistaken for food and are eaten by marine life. Small organisms(plankton) will feed on the broken-down bits of plastic. Which are then be eaten by larger fish and then it is consumed by us.
